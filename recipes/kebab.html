Kebab Recipe

Kebab is a dish consisting of small pieces of meat, tomatoes, onions, etc, threaded onto skewers and grilled, generally over charcoal.

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 small onion, coarsely grated
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced or finely grated
  • 100g fresh breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • sunflower oil for oiling

Method

  1. Marinate the meat
  2. Thread the meat and veggies onto the skewers
  3. Grill on high, direct heat
  4. Let the meat rest for 5 minutes before serving.
